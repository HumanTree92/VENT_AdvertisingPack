[h1]About Velociti Entertainment[/h1]
Velociti Entertainment (VENT or VE) is a PC Gaming Community. We here at VENT mainly focus on Role-Playing & Action but play other types of Genres. We provide a Fun Realistic Role-Play Experience with all the games we play but try keep everything as fun as possible. We have many Positions for players to Rank up in our Gaming Community. You can see what all games we play on our Website. Come check us out & join our Community. If you would like to take part & Rank Up in our Community you can start off by filling out our App on our Forums to become a VE Member.
