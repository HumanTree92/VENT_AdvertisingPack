▼ About Velociti Entertainment ▼
Velociti Entertainment (VENT or VE) is a PC Gaming Community. We here at VENT mainly focus on Role-Playing & Action but play other types of Genres. We provide a Fun Realistic Role-Play Experience with all the games we play but try keep everything as fun as possible. We have many Positions for players to Rank up in our Gaming Community. You can see what all games we play on our Website. Come check us out & join our Community. If you would like to take part & Rank Up in our Community you can start off by filling out our App on our Discord to become a VE Member

▼ Requirements ▼
Mic - This is much needed for easy communications
Steam - Off course this is needed to play games with us
Discord - So we can Communicate easily.
English - This is the preferred language that all of us Speak
Age? - None but if you want to be staff you must be at least 18yrs of age or older
Fun - Must be willing to have fun & have a Good Time

▼ How to become part of VENT ▼
Step 1 - Visit our Discord & Select Here for Community.
Step 2 - Play Games & have Fun!
