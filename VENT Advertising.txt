▼ About Velociti Entertainment ▼
Velociti Entertainment (VENT or VE) is a PC Gaming Community. We keep everything as fun as possible in all the games we play together. We have many Positions for players to Rank up in our Gaming Community.  Come check us out & join our Community. If you would like to take part & Rank Up in our Community you can start off by filling out our App on our Discord to become a VENT Member.
